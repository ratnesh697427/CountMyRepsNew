How to use the Counter

Workout
  1. Select Workout
  2. Click on 'Reps' and input the no. of reps you want
  3. Click on 'Rest' and input the rest time you require between each set
  4. Sets/Time
    • Sets - select the number of sets you wish to perform.
    • Time – Set the duration of the selected workout (E.g. if you’re doing 20-30 pushups for a minute, select 01:00)
  5. Click on 'Start Delay' – select the time (time period) you would like the counter to start your exercise after clicking on the 'start button'. (Pro version only)
  6. Click on 'Counter Interval' – By default it set at '1 second.' Should you wish the counter to count faster or slower than a second, adjust the interval. It will count the Reps faster but does not affect the rest time (Pro version only)
  7. Once you have finished putting in the required information for your exercise and wish to save it, click the 'Add to Favorites' button. (Pro version only)
  8. If you're done with the input and ready to start your exercise, click the 'Start' button at the bottom.
  9. If you want to pause, your exercise, simply click the pause button.
  10. If you want to restart your exercise click the reset button.

 

Breathing
  1. Click on Breathing
  2. Click on ‘Inhale’ – input the required seconds for breathing in
  3. Click on ‘Hold’ – input the required seconds for holding your breath after inhaling
  4. Click on ‘Exhale’ – input the required seconds for Exhaling
  5. Click on ‘Hold’ – input the required seconds for holding your breath after exhaling
  6. Sets/Time
    • Sets - Select the number of sets you wish to perform.
    • Time – Set the duration of the selected Breathing exercise.
  7. Click on ‘Start Delay’ – select the time (time period) you would like the counter to start your exercise after clicking on the ‘start button’ (Pro version only)
  8. Once you have finished putting in the required information for your exercise and wish to save it, click the ‘Add to Favorites’ button. (Pro version only)
  9. If you’re done with the input and ready to start your exercise, click the ‘Start’ button at the bottom.
  10. If you want to pause, your exercise, simply click the pause button.
  11. If you want to restart your exercise, click the reset button.

 

Stopwatch
  1. Click on Stopwatch
  2. To start click the ‘Start’ button at the bottom.
  3. To stop click the ‘Stop’ button at the bottom left.
  4. To resume the stopwatch click the ‘Start’ button at the bottom left (it replaces the 'Stop' button).
  5. To reset the stopwatch click the ‘Reset’ button at the bottom right.

Note: The stopwatch cannot be saved to Favorites

 
How to use Favorites

Save/Create a Favorite (Pro Version Only)
  1. Select the required values in the counter screen (Workout/Breathing)
  2. Scroll down and click on 'Add to Favorites'
  3. Input a name for your exercise and click 'Save'

Note: The stopwatch cannot be saved to Favorites or added to Routines.

 

View/Play Favorites

  1. Select 'Workout' or 'Breathing' from the following: You will see Workout, Breathing, Routines at the top.
  2. To view workouts click on "Workout" and all the workouts added to Favorites will be displayed below. The same shall happen if you click on "Breathing"
  3. If you want to see the details of a Favorite click the details button at the side to view the full information.
  4. To hide the information, click the hide button.
  5. To start a Favorite, simply click on the 'Play' icon to start a saved exercise.

 

Edit/Delete Favorites (Pro Version Only)
  1. If you want to edit a Favorite click on the edit icon and edit the selected icon as per your will. Editing a Favorite item will not affect the Favorite Item added in Routines.
  2. If you want to delete a Favorite click on the delete icon. Deleting a Favorite item will not affect the Favorite Item added in Routines.

 
How to use Routines

Create a Routine (Pro Version Only)
  1. The Routine is created by selecting from Favorites
  2. Go to Favorites
  3. Click on Routines
  4. Select 'Create Routine'
  5. Select any one of the Favorites saved under Workout/Breathing
  6. Select the Interval time (‘Interval - Min Sec’) between each workout at the bottom, just above the ‘Next button’ (e.g. if you want a 30 sec gap after a workout, select ‘SEC’ and change it to ‘30’)
  7. Click the ‘NEXT’ button
  8. Repeat steps "5, 6 and 7" till you have completed selecting the items required for your Routine and click the ‘SAVE’ button.
  9. Name your Routine and click ‘Save’.

 

View/Play Routines

  1. Select 'Routines' from the following: You will see Workout, Breathing, Routines at the top.
  2. If you want to see the details of a Routine click the details button at the side to view the full information.
  3. To hide the information, click the hide button.
  4. To start a Routine, simply click on the 'Play' icon.

 
Settings Page

The settings page can be found on the top right of the 'Counter' Page and on the top left of the 'Favorites' page

  1. To change the voice counter's language of speech, select the language you want from the Voice Language dropdown.
  2. Version – Displays the current version of the app
  3. Instructions – Displays how to use the app
  4. If you want to share this app with your friends click on ‘Share this app’ and choose your preferable social media platform and send.
  5. If you have any feedback, click on ‘Feedback’ and fill in the form accordingly and send it to us. Or visit us at www.countmyrepsapp.com and contact us by clicking the ‘Get in Touch’ button.
